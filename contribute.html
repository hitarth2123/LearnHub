Contribute to LearnHub

Last updated: May 29, 2025

Welcome to the LearnHub community! Your contributions help make LearnHub a valuable resource for learners worldwide. Whether you're an expert in a particular field or just getting started, there are many ways to contribute.

Why Contribute?

  • Share your knowledge with millions of learners worldwide
  • Improve existing content and fix inaccuracies
  • Help maintain the quality and reliability of information
  • Be part of a global community of educators and learners
  • Enhance your own understanding by teaching others

How to Get Started

Create an Account

Sign up for a free LearnHub account to start contributing. It only takes a minute!

Complete the Tutorial

Take our quick tutorial to learn how to edit articles and follow our style guidelines.

Make Your First Edit

Start with small improvements like fixing typos or adding citations to existing articles.

Join the Community

Connect with other contributors, ask questions, and collaborate on projects.

Ways to Contribute

Edit Articles

Improve existing content by fixing errors, adding citations, or updating information.

Create New Articles

Write new articles on topics that haven't been covered yet or need expansion.

Add Media

Upload or create images, diagrams, and other media to enhance articles.

Translate

Help translate articles into different languages to make LearnHub accessible worldwide.

Report Issues

Let us know about any problems you find, from broken links to incorrect information.

Mentor Others

Help new contributors learn the ropes and improve their editing skills.

Content Guidelines

To maintain quality and consistency, please follow these guidelines when contributing:

  • Write from a neutral point of view
  • Provide reliable, verifiable sources for all factual claims
  • Respect copyright and only use content that can be freely distributed
  • Be respectful and collaborative with other contributors
  • Avoid promotional content or advertising

Join our community of contributors today and help us create the world's most comprehensive learning resource.
